Welcome!

I am a Research Fellow in economics at the University of Venice, working under Prof. Agar Brugiavini within the Age-It project.

I hold an MSc in Economics and Political Economy (110/110 cum laude) from the University of Bologna and a Bachelor's in Economics and Finance from the University of Modena and Reggio Emilia.

Research interests: applied microeconomics, industrial organization, health economics, mathematical economics, and game theory.

Current projects include strategic models of product differentiation and the socioeconomic determinants of mortality using SHARE longitudinal data.
